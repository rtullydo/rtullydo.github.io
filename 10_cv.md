# Curriculum Vitae 

### Research Interests

- Functional analysis, operator theory, [operator analysis](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/operator-analysis/AC63E45A1B026B89321E3159287AAF11), free noncommutative analysis
- Undergraduate mathematical programming, mathematical modeling
